Do you have exam anxiety?

Students may experience stress or anxiety before an exam. The anxiety may range from "knots in your stomach" to a full blown panic attack. The stress and anxiety associated with an exam may be debilitating and can intefere with a student's exam performance or daily life. This is why it is particularly important to recognize the symptoms and deal with them as quickly and efficiently as possible.

Symptoms of exam anxiety

Exam anxiety may manifest in a variety of forms and expressions including physical, emotional and cognitive. Symptoms may include, but are not limited to the following:

  • Sweating
  • Dry mouth
  • Rapid heartbeat
  • Nausea
  • Vomiting
  • Diarrhea
  • Depression
  • Anger e.g. "outbursts"
  • Feeling worthless
  • Forgetfulness e.g. "blanking-out"

Need help? No problem!
